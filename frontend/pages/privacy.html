Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • YouTube video URLs you submit for download
  • Download preferences (quality, format)
  • Technical information (browser type, device type)
  • Usage analytics (page views, feature usage)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process your download requests
  • Improve our service performance
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize features
  • Communicate service updates when necessary

3. Data Storage

Downloaded files are temporarily stored on our servers for the duration of the download process and are automatically deleted within 1 hour after completion. We do not permanently store any downloaded content.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, gather general visitor information, and track visits to our website. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and data stored on our servers.

7. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@ytultra.com
